About the founder

Arronv_ecoclub was created in June of 2020 by Aida Ongarova - 15-year-old girl. The inspiration for the eco-club came when her allergy worsened because of poor air quality in Nur-Sultan. So, she diceded to clean streets and plant more trees. Her goals for following 4 years are planting at least 25 trees and supporting recycling stations by preventing their closing.
