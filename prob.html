Eco-club in Nur-Sultan.

Kazakhstan possesses vast territory and rich natural resources. Just like any country nowadays Kazakhstan focuses on socio-economic development which negatively impacts on the environment. The worst environmental issues in Kazakhstan:

  • Radiation from The Semipalatinsk Nuclear Test Site.
  • The shrinking of the Aral sea and Balkhash lake.
  • Air polution in Almaty and Nur-Sultan.
  • Recycling.
  • Urban pollution.

How can I help ecology, if I live in Nur-Sultan?

Our club is noncommercial organisation dedicated on solving urban pollution in Nur-Sultan. Together we clean streets, parks and shores of Esil river. Also, every Sunday we do a phone call meeting on WhatsApp, where we rate how ecologically friendly we spend our week, share usefull information about zero-waste life style and arrange following cleanings meetings.
